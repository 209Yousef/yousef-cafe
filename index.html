About the restaurant

Welcome to Yousef Café! We are a cozy and friendly café located in the heart of the city. Our mission is to offer delicious food made with fresh ingredients and served with a smile.

Type of food and atmosphere of the place

At Yousef Café, we specialize in a variety of dishes including international cuisine, pastries, and refreshing beverages. Enjoy our warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for family gatherings, friends, or a relaxing coffee break.

Explore our delicious menu, check out our special dishes, and enjoy a cozy dining experience!

About

The story of the restaurant fouding

Yousef Café was founded in 2020 with the dream of creating a warm and welcoming place for food lovers. From a small cozy space, it has grown into a popular spot where people come to enjoy delicious dishes and a friendly atmosphere.

Service delivery steps :

  1. Greet customers with a warm welcome.
  2. Present the menu and explain daily specials.
  3. Take the customer order carefully.
  4. Prepare and serve the dishes with attention to detail.
  5. Ensure customer satisfaction and invite them to visit again.

Menu

  • Grilled Chicken Salad – Fresh lettuce, cherry tomatoes, grilled chicken, olive oil dressing.
  • Spaghetti Carbonara – Pasta, eggs, parmesan cheese, bacon, black pepper.
  • Margherita Pizza – Tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil.
